Welcome to Halloween Hootenanny

Get ready to experience creepy, crawly, eerie, ghoulish and frightening Halloween fun in Minneapolis during 27th-31st October, 2020!! The Great Pumpkin Walk

Trick-or-Treating on Halloween Night

October 31st; 6 pm to 9 pm
a Halloween ritual custom for children and adults. The "treat" is usually some form of candy, although in some cultures money is given instead. The "trick" refers to a threat, usually idle, to perform mischief on the homeowner or their property if no treat is given.

Wabasha Street Cave Tours

Lost Souls Tour: 27th-28th October
Ghosts & Graves: 29th-30th October
Get in the Halloween Spirit with Wabasha Street Cave tours of walking and bus tours that include stories of haunted sites, murders and cave visits.
